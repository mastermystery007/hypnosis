# Practical Hypnosis — Production Manifest

The course text, scripts, drills, troubleshooting, quizzes, bookmarks and completion tracking are implemented. The items below are the remaining original media and release assets.

## Required store assets

- `assets/icon.png` — 1024×1024, dark violet identity, no small text.
- `assets/adaptive-icon.png` — Android foreground with safe central symbol.
- `assets/splash.png` — 1284×2778 portrait splash.
- `store/feature-graphic.png` — 1024×500.
- Six portrait phone screenshots showing curriculum, lesson theory, model wording, quiz, completion progress and safety boundary.
- Privacy-policy public URL and support email before store submission.

## Recording standard

- Video: 1080p, 30 fps, landscape master plus portrait crop where practical.
- Audio: 48 kHz WAV master; export 192 kbps MP3/AAC for the app.
- Demonstrations must show clear informed consent, stable posture, cancellation and debriefing.
- Do not include clinical claims, surprise inductions, unsafe falls, neck manipulation, humiliation or coercive suggestions.
- Obtain written model releases from every visible participant.

## Content and attribution rules

Use original narration, demonstrations, diagrams and scripts. Research may draw from established hypnosis literature, but no passage, commercial induction, proprietary course sequence or branded routine should be copied. Add a bibliography page citing books and professional guidance consulted.

## Release acceptance criteria

- All 15 lessons open without network access.
- Completion and bookmarks survive app restart.
- Every quiz marks the correct answer and displays an explanation.
- No media button points to a missing production file in the release build.
- The disclaimer appears in store copy and inside the app.
- At least two external reviewers check participant safety wording.
- Android production build passes installation and navigation tests on a low-memory physical device.
